# 🍔 Food Delivery Analytics Dashboard

A complete Power BI analytics dashboard built to analyze food delivery operations, customer behavior, restaurant performance, and delivery efficiency through interactive visualizations and business intelligence reporting.

---

## 📌 Project Overview

This project transforms raw food delivery transaction data into actionable business insights using Power BI.

The dashboard enables analysis of:

* Order performance
* Revenue generation
* Customer satisfaction
* Delivery efficiency
* Restaurant performance
* Cuisine popularity
* City-wise demand trends

The report is designed with interactive visuals, KPI cards, slicers, and trend analysis to support data-driven decision-making in food delivery businesses.

---

## 📊 Dashboard Pages

### 1. Home Dashboard

The Home Dashboard provides a complete overview of operational and sales performance.

### Key Metrics

* Total Orders: 10K
* Total Revenue: 5M
* Average Delivery Time: 40.12 Minutes
* Average Rating: 4.01
* Late Delivery Percentage: 0.49%

### Visualizations Included

* Monthly Order Trend
* Orders by City
* Orders by Cuisine
* Orders by Rating Groups
* Top Restaurants by Orders
* KPI Summary Cards

### Key Insights

* More than 10,000 orders were analyzed.
* Revenue exceeded 5 million.
* Indian cuisine generated the highest order volume.
* Chennai recorded the highest number of orders.
* Tasty Bites emerged as the top-performing restaurant.
* Customer ratings remained consistently positive across orders.

---

### 2. Delivery Performance Analysis

The Delivery Analysis dashboard focuses on delivery efficiency and service quality.

### Visualizations Included

* Late Delivery Percentage by City
* Average Delivery Time by Month
* Orders vs Late Delivery Percentage
* Delivery Status Distribution

### Key Insights

* Over 90% of orders were successfully delivered.
* Cancellation rates remained below 10%.
* Delivery performance was relatively consistent across cities.
* Average delivery time stayed around 40 minutes throughout the year.
* Late deliveries represented a very small percentage of total orders.
